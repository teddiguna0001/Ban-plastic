Why Should We Ban Plastic?

Traditional plastic takes hundreds of years to decompose. It pollutes oceans, kills wildlife, and enters our food chain as microplastics.

Every year, over 8 million tons of plastic waste enters the ocean.

What is Algae-Based Organic Plastic?

Algae-based plastic is 100% biodegradable, non-toxic, and sustainable. It decomposes naturally in weeks instead of centuries.

  • Algae plastic reduces carbon emissions by 70%.
  • It is safe for marine life and soil-friendly.
  • Production requires no harmful chemicals.

How You Can Help

  • Stop using single-use plastics.
  • Support algae-based biodegradable products.
  • Spread awareness in your school or community.
  • Encourage companies to adopt green alternatives.
